About Us

Mission

  • To be an Ambassador for Philippine Visual Arts.
  • To educate and integrate art appreciation to the pass-on generation.
  • Develop local artists with passion to create and innovate art..

Vision

To promote and develop Philippine Visual Arts. The gallery aims to be a creative ground where visitors and artists can share a visual conversation through art appreciation, to enrich their cultural experience and strengthen nationalism.
