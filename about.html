About Us

Both are freelance visual artists who are facinated by something old and something modern in combination.

Mission

Many incredibly talented painters are unnoticed because they do not have a better place to expose their artistic skills in public.This website was created to allow the artist to showcase their masterpiece and at the same time, it will allow the artist to promote their artwork. The gallery offers viewers and collectors a direct relationship with the painters.


Vision

Every artist must have a place to showcase their talent. This website was created to bring inspiration and innovation to other people who possess the same art skills and get a better chance to work with the artist to collaborate various art skills in a single artwork.
